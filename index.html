1. Introduction

Tridev Innovation ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Tridev Attendance mobile application (the "Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and password when you create an account
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) to verify your physical location during attendance check-in
  • Work Information: Work schedule times, attendance records, check-in/check-out times, and leave requests
  • Professional Details: Job title, department, and employee ID (if applicable)
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support inquiries

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Features used, interaction patterns, time spent in app, and performance metrics
  • Camera Access: QR code data only (we do NOT store photos or videos)
  • App Crash Data: Error logs and crash reports for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Authenticating users and maintaining attendance records
  • Location Verification: Ensuring employees are physically present at the office for check-in
  • Attendance Management: Recording, calculating, and reporting work hours and attendance
  • Leave Management: Processing and tracking leave requests and approvals
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements for employment records
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve application features and performance
  • Communication: Sending important updates about your account and service changes
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities and unauthorized access
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with our application

4. Permissions We Request

4.1 Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To scan QR codes for attendance verification
  • Data Collected: QR code data only - we do NOT collect or store personal photos
  • Data Retention: QR code data is processed immediately and not stored
  • User Control: You can deny this permission; the app will not function for QR-based check-in

4.2 Location (GPS) Permission

  • Purpose: To verify your physical location during check-in/check-out
  • Data Collected: Latitude and longitude coordinates with timestamp
  • Accuracy: Approximate location (±100 meters) is sufficient for office verification
  • Data Retention: Location is recorded with attendance timestamps for compliance
  • User Control: You can deny this permission; check-in may not be allowed without location

4.3 Storage Access Permission

  • Purpose: To export and share attendance reports in CSV format
  • Data Collected: Access to read/write files for export functionality only
  • Data Retention: Exported files are stored on your device; we do not retain copies
  • User Control: You can deny this permission; report export feature will be disabled

4.4 Internet Permission

  • Purpose: To communicate with our servers for all application features
  • Data Transmitted: All data is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • User Control: Required for app functionality
Note: We only request permissions necessary for our app's functionality. You can manage permissions in your Android settings for each permission independently.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS with TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • Secure Storage: Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device
  • Password Security: Passwords are never stored locally and are securely transmitted
  • No Plain Text: Sensitive data is never stored in plaintext
  • Regular Security Updates: We regularly update our systems to address security vulnerabilities
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access your data

5.2 Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your password confidential
  • Do not share your account credentials with others
  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Report any unauthorized access immediately

5.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 30 days
  • Notify relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate corrective measures
  • Provide guidance on protecting your information

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Purpose
Authentication Tokens Until logout or 90 days of inactivity User session management
Attendance Records 3 years or as required by law Legal compliance and record-keeping
Location Data With attendance records (3 years) Compliance and audit trail
Leave Records 3 years or as required by law Legal compliance

Error Logs 30 days Troubleshooting and improvements

Note: You can request deletion of your account at any time, which will trigger deletion of associated personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this information within 30 days.

7.2 Right to Correction

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. You can update most information directly in the app.

7.3 Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten), subject to legal retention requirements. Once your account is deleted, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

7.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to request your data in a portable format (CSV) that you can use with other services.

7.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to withdraw consent for data processing at any time by contacting us.
